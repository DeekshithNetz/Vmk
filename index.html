About UsA School That Feels Like Family

Where every child is known by name, and every family is part of our community.

Founded in 2010 with a simple belief — that every child deserves to be seen, heard, and nurtured — Vijaya Makkala Koota has grown from a small neighborhood school into one of the most trusted CBSE institutions in Bangalore.

Today, we serve over 1800 students from LKG through Grade 10. Our classrooms are bright and inviting, our teachers are deeply invested, and our campus is designed so children feel safe, curious, and happy every single day.

We don't just prepare students for exams. We prepare them for life — with empathy, confidence, and the ability to think for themselves.
