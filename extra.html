Beyond the Classroom Extracurricular Activities

Nurturing talent, building confidence, and preserving our cultural heritage through diverse activities.

Traditional

Yakshagana

Our traditional Yakshagana training preserves Karnataka's rich folk art heritage. Students learn the art of expression through dance, dialogue, and elaborate costumes.

Grade 3 – 10
Classical Dance

Bharatanatyam

Students master the fundamentals of Bharatanatyam including adavus, mudras, and abhinaya. Regular performances help build stage confidence and grace.

Grade 1 – Grade 10
Contemporary

Western Dance

From hip-hop to contemporary, our Western dance classes teach rhythm, coordination, and creative expression. Students perform at school events and competitions.

Grade 1 – 10
Music

Singing

Vocal training covers classical, semi-classical, and light music. Students develop breath control, pitch accuracy, and stage presence through regular practice.

Grade 1 – Grade 10
Martial Arts

Karate

Our certified karate program teaches self-defense, discipline, and respect. Students progress through belt levels and participate in district and state championships.

Grade 1 – 10
Devotional

Bhajana Singing

Students learn traditional devotional songs and keerthanes, connecting with our spiritual roots. Regular bhajana sessions foster community bonding and inner peace.

All Grades

Give Your Child the Gift of Holistic Growth

Our extracurricular activities complement academic learning and help students become confident, well-rounded individuals.
