Our Story

From a Small Dream to a Thriving Community

Vijaya Makkala Koota was founded in 2012 with a simple yet powerful belief — that every child deserves to be seen, heard, and nurtured. What began as a small neighborhood school with just 80 students and 6 teachers has grown into one of the most trusted institutions in Byndoor Taluk.

The name translates to "Victorious Children's Association" — a name that reflects our deepest aspiration: to see every child leave as a confident, capable, and compassionate young person.

Today, we serve over 800 students from Grade 1 through Grade 10 with smart classrooms, equipped laboratories, a sprawling library, dedicated sports facilities, and safe transport — all designed so children feel secure, curious, and happy every single day.
