Frequently Asked Common Questions

Quick answers to what parents ask most often.

What is the admission process?

Submit an enquiry form on this page or call us. Our admissions team will schedule an interaction session. For Grade 1–Grade 1, we assess readiness through a gentle observation. For Grade 2 and above, there's a simple age-appropriate assessment. Documents required include birth certificate, previous school TC (if applicable), address proof, and passport-size photos.

What are the school fees?

Fee details vary by grade and are shared during the admissions interaction. We offer flexible payment plans — annual, semi-annual, and quarterly options. Sibling discounts are available. Please contact our office for the complete fee structure.

Is transport available?

Yes, we operate GPS-tracked school buses covering 15+ routes across Jayanagar, JP Nagar, BTM Layout, Banashankari, and surrounding areas. All buses have female attendants and first-aid kits. Route details and pickup timings are shared at the time of admission.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer art, music, dance (Bharatanatyam and folk), yoga, karate, cricket, basketball, badminton, and athletics. Students also participate in science clubs, quiz competitions, and cultural events throughout the year. Activities are integrated into the weekly timetable from Grade 1 onwards.

What safety measures are in place?

The campus has 60+ CCTV cameras, guarded entry/exit with visitor management system, female attendants on every floor and in washrooms, regular fire safety drills, and a school counsellor. All staff undergo background verification. Pick-up is through ID cards only — no child is handed over without proper verification.

Can I visit the campus before applying?

Absolutely! We encourage parents to visit before making a decision. Campus visits are conducted on weekdays between 9:30 AM and 12:30 PM. Call us at +91 80 1234 5678 or submit the enquiry form above to schedule your visit.

What is the medium of instruction?

English is the medium of instruction for all subjects from Grade 1 to Grade 10. Kannada and Hindi are taught as second and third languages as per CBSE guidelines. We also place emphasis on spoken English from the early years.
